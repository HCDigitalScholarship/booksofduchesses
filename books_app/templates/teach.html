Teaching Materials


Books of Duchesses can be used in the classroom to develop student research skills. The project can be used to support student learning in courses on book history, medieval and early modern literature, and gender studies.

For an overview of how to use Books of Duchesses in the classroom please explore this video introduction on Princeton's Middle Ages for Educators website.
